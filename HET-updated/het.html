Our Services

The vision of our founding fathers has been to create a society that is heart-centred. Our services below are stepping stones towards that vision:

Education

Specialized programs for cognitive development, faculty development & leadership training

Yoga & wellness

We have a meditative approach to Yoga that allows us to explore the physical, mental and spiritual all at once

Agriculture & Nursery

We aspire to create a green tomorrow by bringing in a perfect amalgamation between age old growth wisdom and latest propagation techniques like hydroponics, tissue culture and much more

Mechanical & civil

Sustainable constructions have been the benchmark of our spaces, come learn the latest technologies in eco-friendly construction, reuse, renew, recycle and much more

What we Offers

Education

Heartfulness Education enables teachers and students to live a balanced, purposeful, and a happy life using heart-based practices, so that they are equipped to contribute towards a sustainable school reform and community citizenship.

Health & Wellness

Make a real change in your wellbeing by gaining awareness in preventive health-care with a retreat including movement, nutrition, life-style guidance and meditation techniques

Civil & Construction

The latest technologies in green construction for a sustainable tomorrow

Mechnical & Automotive

Sustainability in action truly by creating, sustaining, re-using and re-cycling resources

Yoga

We have a meditative approach to Yoga that allows us to explore the physical, mental and spiritual all at once. Heartfulness Yoga brings you this full spectrum of Yoga by creating a seamless flow between asanas and meditation

Agriculture & Nursery

With a need to consume naturally grown food and have it healthy, we started becoming organic and in this process we learnt the art of being kind to mother earth

IT & Digital Technologies

Equipping the future generations with digital possibilities to collaborate and build the next cool things

Logistics & supply Chain

The best practices in supply-chain management
